Net Art Project

Here is my Net Art that I created using HTML coding!

This button will take you to go on this adventure: Click Me!

For this project, I decided to make a choose your own adventure type story. My main character, Mush, is looking for their good friend, Lily, and asks you to help find her. You meet different individuals (all characters from Animal Crossing because I love Animal Crossing LOL) based on who you choose to go ask, and regardless of the path you choose Mush and Lily eventually reunite. Just a fun little journey :)
