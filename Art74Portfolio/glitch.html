Glitch Art Project

Here are my glitch art pieces that I created using TextEdit and Audacity!

For this glitch of a forest, I used the text edit method. I decided to glitch this image to comment on how forests are being cut down, taking away the habitats of many animals.

For this glitch of a frog, I used the echo effect in Audacity. I decided to glitch this image to comment on how animals live under constant threat of global warming. When I put the image back into Photoshop from Audacity, it turned the image upside down, which I found fitting for my message.

For this glitch of the ocean, I used the phaser effect in Audacity. I decided to glitch this image to comment on the pollution of the ocean. When I opened the image again in Photoshop, it was also turned upside down which fits the message.
